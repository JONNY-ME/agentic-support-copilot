# FAQ (English) — Addis Market Support Copilot Demo

Last updated: 2026-02-10

## 1) Delivery coverage

**Q: Do you deliver to Bole?**  
A: Yes. We deliver to Bole and most central Addis Ababa areas.

**Q: Which areas do you deliver to in Addis Ababa?**  
A: We deliver to:
- Bole (including Megenagna, Gerji, CMC)
- Kazanchis
- Piassa / Arada
- Mexico / Kirkos
- Lideta
- Saris / Nifas Silk (selected zones)

If you are not sure about your area, tell us your neighborhood name and a nearby landmark.

## 2) Delivery fees and timing

**Q: How much is delivery?**  
A: Delivery depends on your area:
- Central Addis Ababa: 120 ETB
- Outer zones: 180 ETB  
Fees may change during peak hours or special promos.

**Q: How long does delivery take?**  
A: Typical delivery times:
- Same-day delivery: 3 to 6 hours (for orders placed before 2:00 PM)
- Next-day delivery: for orders placed after 2:00 PM

**Q: Do you offer scheduled delivery?**  
A: Yes. You can request a preferred time window (for example 10:00–12:00 or 2:00–5:00). We will confirm availability.

## 3) Order status and tracking

**Q: How do I check my order status?**  
A: Send your order id (example: **ETH-1001**) and we will check the latest status.

**Q: What if I do not have my order id?**  
A: Share your phone number and approximate order time with a human agent, or request a callback. For privacy, do not post sensitive information in public groups.

**Q: Common order statuses**  
- processing: we received your order and are preparing it  
- shipped: your order is on the way  
- delivered: your order was delivered  
- cancelled: your order was cancelled

## 4) Changes, cancellations, and failed deliveries

**Q: Can I change my order after placing it?**  
A: Yes, if the order is still in “processing”. Message us quickly and include your order id.

**Q: Can I cancel my order?**  
A: You can cancel before the order is shipped. If it is already shipped, cancellation may not be possible.

**Q: What if delivery fails?**  
A: If we cannot reach you, we may:
- attempt delivery again once, or
- hold the package for pickup, depending on the situation.

## 5) Returns, refunds, and replacements

**Q: What is your return policy?**  
A: Returns depend on product type:
- Sealed, non-perishable items: return within 7 days if unopened
- Perishable items: no returns unless damaged or incorrect
- Damaged or incorrect items: report within 24 hours for replacement review

**Q: How do I report a damaged item?**  
A: Send your order id, the item name, and a short description. A human agent may request photos.

## 6) Payments (safety)

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: Common options include:
- Cash on delivery
- Telebirr
- Bank transfer (if available)

**Important safety note:**  
Never share card numbers, PINs, OTP codes, or passwords in chat. If anyone asks for these, request a human agent.

## 7) Product info and pricing

**Q: Can you send the price list?**  
A: Yes. Ask for the product category you want (coffee, tea, honey, spices), or request the full catalog.

**Q: Are prices final?**  
A: Prices can change based on availability and promotions. The final price is confirmed at checkout or when your order is confirmed.

## 8) Support and escalation

**Q: How do I open a support ticket?**  
A: Describe the issue and we will create a ticket and share the ticket id.

**Q: Can I talk to a human agent?**  
A: Yes. Say “human agent” and we will escalate your request.

**Q: Can you call me back?**  
A: Yes. Say “call me” and we will schedule a callback time window.
